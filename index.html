Investments Schemes

  • Public Provident Fund

    PPF

    • Availability - Available at almost all Indian banks and post offices.
    • Investment Amount - Minimum investment amount is INR 500 per annum.
    • Maturity - A PPF fund matures in a span of 15 years.
    • Taxation - Investment in PPF is tax-free.

  • Sovereign Gold Bonds

    SGBs

    • Availability - SBGs are open for auction on dates announced by the central government. These bonds are issued by the RBI multiple times a year.
    • Investment Amount - Each bond unit you purchase has the value of one gram of pure gold based on gold’s average closing price of the previous three business days.
    • Return on Investment - 2.5% paid twice a year.
    • Maturity - Eight years. Early redemption after five years.

  • Initial Public Offerings

    IPO

    • Availability - IPOs are accessible to retail investors through brokerage firms and online trading platforms.
    • Investment Amount - The investment amount required for participating in an IPO can vary significantly, depending on the company and its pricing.
    • Maturity - IPO investments typically do not have a lock-in period.
    • Return - Returns on IPO investments can be substantial, especially if the company experiences significant growth after going public.

  • Gold Exchange-Traded Funds

    ETFS

    • Availability - You can buy units of gold by opening a demat account, exactly in the same manner that one invests in shares from the stock brokerage companies and agencies registered with SEBI.
    • Investment Amount- A minimum of one unit, which is equivalent to a gram of pure gold, is recommended.
    • Maturity - As the price of gold increases, the value of your unit will also increase, and vice versa.
    • Return on Investment - Just like an equity mutual fund, ETFs can also be traded on stock exchanges.
    • Taxation - If you sell your gold ETF before 36 months of acquiring it, then you will be taxed as per your slab. After 36 months, long-term capital gains tax of 20% plus 4% cess is applicable.
